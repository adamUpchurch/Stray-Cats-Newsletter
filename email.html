What did you miss on Product Hunt?

There's a lot of gold on Product Hunt, whether it's a neat little do this app or the New Macbook Pro. But it's hard to keep up and especially so to get through to the diamonds in the rough. That's what this is for, The Stray Cat Project. A daily newsletter of projects that didn't get the love they deserved on Product Hunt.
